## ADDED Requirements
### Requirement: Opt-In AI Assistance
The system SHALL provide AI assistance only when the user explicitly opts in.

#### Scenario: Enable AI assistance
- **WHEN** a user opts in to AI assistance
- **THEN** the system SHALL allow AI-generated summaries for their data

### Requirement: Non-Advice Disclaimers
The system SHALL display non-advice disclaimers alongside AI outputs.

#### Scenario: View AI summary
- **WHEN** a user views an AI summary
- **THEN** the system SHALL display a non-advice disclaimer
