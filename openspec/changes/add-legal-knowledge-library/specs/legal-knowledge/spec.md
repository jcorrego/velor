## ADDED Requirements
### Requirement: Legal Document Metadata
The system SHALL store official legal documents with metadata and effective dates.

#### Scenario: Add legal document metadata
- **WHEN** a user adds an official document
- **THEN** the system SHALL store jurisdiction, topics, and effective dates

### Requirement: Legal Document Search
The system SHALL provide search and filtering over legal documents.

#### Scenario: Search by topic
- **WHEN** a user searches for a topic
- **THEN** the system SHALL return documents with matching topics
