# Change: Add Form 5472 year-end totals summary card

## Why
Form 5472 summary cards currently omit year-end asset and account totals needed for a complete end-of-year snapshot.

## What Changes
- Add a Form 5472 summary card showing the sum of year-end assets and accounts for the selected filing year.
- Scope totals to entities in the same jurisdiction as the selected filing.
- Display totals grouped by entity within that jurisdiction.

## Impact
- Affected specs: us-tax-reporting
- Affected code: Form 5472 summary UI, US tax reporting aggregation services
